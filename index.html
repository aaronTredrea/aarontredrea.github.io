About Me

Product-first.
Detail-obsessed.

Freelance iOS developer with 16 years of experience, working across both ambitious startups and established product teams. I specialise in taking ideas from early concept through to successful App Store launch, as well as scaling, optimising, and evolving mature apps - including products with 100M+ downloads worldwide.

With experience spanning early-stage ventures to large, stable codebases, I understand when to move fast and when to prioritise long-term maintainability. I'm comfortable operating at both ends of the spectrum - from rapid prototyping and zero-to-one builds to improving performance, architecture, and user experience in widely used products.

I've fully embraced AI development as a core part of my workflow, using agentic engineering patterns and tools like Claude, Cursor, and ChatGPT to dramatically accelerate output across code generation, architecture decisions, debugging, and documentation. Backed by 16 years of experience, I critically evaluate and refine AI-generated solutions rather than relying on them blindly.

Whether working independently or leading distributed teams, I bring a product-first mindset, strong UI/UX instincts, and a rigorous approach to building clean, scalable systems - alongside a commitment to staying ahead of emerging tools, frameworks, and AI-driven development practices.
